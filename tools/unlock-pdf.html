What is Unlock PDF?

Unlock PDF is a free online tool that removes password protection from PDF files you already have access to. By entering the correct password, you can generate an unrestricted version of the PDF that can be opened, shared, and printed without entering a password each time.

The entire process runs locally in your web browser ‚Äî no files or passwords are ever sent to a server. This makes it one of the safest ways to remove PDF restrictions while keeping your sensitive documents completely private.

This tool is intended for use with PDF files you own or have the legal right to access. It requires you to know and enter the existing password ‚Äî it does not crack or bypass passwords.
