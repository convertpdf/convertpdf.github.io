Why Convert Excel to PDF?

Excel spreadsheets are powerful for data analysis and calculation, but they aren't ideal for sharing. Recipients need Microsoft Excel or a compatible program to open .xlsx files, and formatting can change dramatically depending on the viewer's software version.

Converting Excel to PDF solves these problems instantly. PDFs display identically on every device and operating system, preserve your exact layout and formatting, and prevent accidental edits to your data. They're also much easier to print.

Our free Excel to PDF converter processes your file entirely in your browser, so your financial data, business reports, or personal budgets never leave your device ‚Äî a critical feature for sensitive spreadsheets.
